Kia Ora, Welcome

I'm a PhD candidate in Software Engineering at the University of Auckland. My current research focuses on crowd sourcing software requirements (CrowdRE), often leveraging machine learning and natural language processing techniques. A full list of my published work can be found under publications. I'm also part of the Human Aspects of Software Engineering Lab (HASEL), check it out at https://hasel.auckland.ac.nz.

For a more reader-friendly version of my research I've started a blog on Medium, check out this 3 minute article discussing who actually writes reviews on the app store? In 2022 I'll be taking on an exciting new challenge working as a teaching fellow at the University of Auckland. My teaching will focus on software requirements, and development methodologies.

If you're interested in my research, I'd love to hear from you at jtiz003@aucklanduni.ac.nz. If you'd like to build your own website, see this easy to follow template on github. Thanks for visiting my site!
